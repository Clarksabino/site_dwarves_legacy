The World & Lore

Game Synopsis

In a world buried in darkness, the ancient dwarven mines have become a labyrinth of corruption and echoes of a once-glorious past. The Mirror of Desires, an artifact of incomparable power, seduced an entire people with promises of wealth and abundance—only to enslave their souls to the Lord of Reflection, a demon that feeds on illusions.

You are a lone adventurer who ventures into these forgotten mines, where corrupted dwarves roam as hollow reflections and reality itself seems twisted. To survive, you must seek the magical hats of the seven fallen heroes, each granting unique abilities and fragments of a lost history.

Grow stronger, confront creatures shaped by illusion, and unravel the secrets of the inverted world. Only by reclaiming the relic that fuels the Sacred Forge can the cycle of deception be broken—restoring hope to the dwarven people… or condemning the mines forever to an eternal reflection.
